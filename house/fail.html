The Foreman

If he shows up, you have 1 round to decide whether to ENGAGE (use the Combat table to compare your PHY against his) and waste 1 hour or test INT E to ESCAPE and waste 24 hours.

Success

If you suceeded the test, you either ESCAPED or DEFEATED the foreman; it's up to you decide what to do with him, but you can be assured he wont's show up again. Also, you now can use his keys and ignore all locked doors and chests. Choose where to go: [01] [04] [05] [11] [12] [18].

Fail

If you fail either engage or escape, the group will be imprisoned in the basement and delivered to the police to be arrested for 72 hours until Dr. Van Helsing shows up and bails you out. That also means Dr. Montgomery is back and this part of the mission failed .

Fail Miserabily

If you draw the Sad Mask, the group will be imprisoned in the basement and delivered to the police. Dr. Van Helsing was also arrested and you will need a lawyer .
