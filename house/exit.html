Witchcraft Tales: The House

The timer will stop at 21 minutes and 36 seconds. That is equal to 3 hours and 36 seconds inside this room, from the 72 hours total you have in the house before Dr. Montgomery returns. If you finish exploring the room before that, stop the timer and take note so that you keep track of time. If you need more time in the room, reset the timer and take note of how long you spent.

If you found evidence against both, go to The Theater. You earned 3 points of Management and 1 point of positive Valor.



If you found evidence only against the baroness, go to The Theater. You earned 2 points of Management.
If you found evidence only against the doctor, go to The Cemetery or to The Theater. You earned 1 point of Management.



If you failed, you gain 1 point of negative Valor. Tell your tales.
