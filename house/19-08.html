The Mapinguari

If anyone dares to come closer to the surgery table they will be able to notice the body of the BIG MAN [!] is not in fact dead and stands up, placing himself between you and the iron safe. It ATTACKS with surprising strength and speed (PHY +6) for a body in such state. Also, believers must check the cards below.

Success

If you defeat the opponents, you find where the evidences are hidden [$]. However, you need to unlock the safe without triggering the trap inside. If you agreed and succeded to help the ashoolmaster's spirit in this room, he will tell you the combination. If not, TEST Security + INT H or Crafts + INT VH: . If you got a stroke of luck , you gain one extra hour in the house. Anyway, you can go back [18]. However, if you think you have enough evidence, you may leave the house .

Fail

If you fail either engage or escape, the group will be imprisoned in the basement and delivered to the police to be arrested for 72 hours until Dr. Van Helsing shows up and bails you out. That also means Dr. Montgomery is back and this part of the mission failed .

Fail Miserabily

If you draw the Sad Mask, the group will be imprisoned in the basement and delivered to the police. Dr. Van Helsing was also arrested and you will need a lawyer. Tell your Tales.
