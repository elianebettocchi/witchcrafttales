Witchcraft Tales [17] Attic

The Witch

Immediately, what it seems to be two women and a men [!] fully wrapped in bloody and gooey bandages, who seem to be in a sort of hypnotic trance, attack. They seem to be protecting a white witch-like WOMAN [!] with long shaggy silver hair. Before the attack, you have 1 round to decide whether to ENGAGE (use the combat table) and waste 1 hour or test INT O to ESCAPE and waste 24 hours. Also, BELIEVERS must check the cards below. Whatever the outcome, you notice the 3 “corpses” are actually alive but catatonic. They are in very bad shape and in need of urgent medical assistance. Otherwise, they will die for real in 24 hours.

Success

If you defeat the opponents, you find where the evidences are hidden [$]. However, you need to unlock the casket without triggering the trap inside. If you agreed and succeded to help the lady's spirit in room 07, the Saci will tell you the combination. If not, TEST Security + INT H or Crafts + INT VH: . If you got a stroke of luck , you gain one extra hour in the house. Anyway, you can go back DOWN [16] the stairs. However, if you think you have enough evidence, you may leave the house .

Fail

If you fail either engage or escape, the group will be imprisoned in the basement and delivered to the police to be arrested for 72 hours until Dr. Van Helsing shows up and bails you out. That also means Dr. Montgomery is back and this part of the mission failed .

Fail Miserabily

If you draw the Sad Mask, the group will be imprisoned in the basement and delivered to the police. Dr. Van Helsing was also arrested and you will need a lawyer .
